Contrivity

Spawn into the hostile quantum laboratory and destroy oscillations in this endless, top-down, twin-stick arena shooting game.
Play in the browser or download a copy now.

Game

Controls

Use arrow keys to shoot and w a s d keys to move.

Instructions

Avoid and shoot the advancing enemy oscillations.

Activate powerful weapons by staying within the pulsing circle that moves every 5 seconds.

Play

Screen size is too small to play.

Explanation

Contrivity is designed as a fun, minimalist twin-stick shooting game with a unique proximity-based weapon activation system.

It's optimized heavily using JavaScript, HTML and CSS without a game engine or WebGL.

All assets used are original and scalable to any 16:9 resolution greater than or equal to 672x378.

It doesn't use cookies or connectivity to an API over the internet.
